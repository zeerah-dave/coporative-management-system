Transactions Overview
Members are to note the following:

1. There is no charge for deposits made.

2. For withdrawals, NGN {{ withdrawal_charge }} will be charged from member's account. This is done automatically by the system.

3. NGN {{ loan_charge }} will be charged for every loan collected. This is also done automatically by the system.

4. A flat interest rate of {{ interest }} % is used across board.

5. For purchases made on credit, the duration for payment is also calculated automatically by the system.

6. Note that there will be no refund of charges after approval of any transaction.

7. The price per unit share is NGN {{ unit_share }}. Members are not charged for buying shares.
